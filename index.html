THE PROBLEM

Competitive intelligence in biopharma is broken

  • CI teams manually pull data from dozens of disconnected sources
  • Insights arrive weeks or months after competitive events
  • Analysts spend 80% of time on data wrangling, not analysis
  • No single view across Medical Affairs, Commercial, Market Access, Clinical, Regulatory, and Patient Advocacy
OUR SOLUTION

Always-on AI agents that do the work for you

  • Automated ingestion from public data sources, updated continuously
  • Governed metrics across all six biopharma functions via a semantic layer
  • AI agents that surface insights, not just dashboards that display data
  • Built on a compliance-first architecture — bright-line rules enforced at the data layer

COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORKArchitectural Enforcement

What We Measure

  • Clinical knowledge diffusion velocity
  • Guideline adoption and evidence uptake
  • Evidence generation cadence across assets
  • Payer decision acceleration signals
  • Competitive positioning relative to launch

What We Never Measure

  • Prescribing volume attribution to field activity
  • Revenue-to-engagement causation
  • MSL or MA compensation links
  • Individual prescriber behavior changes
  • Sales targeting effectiveness

These constraints are enforced architecturally at the semantic layer, not just via prompts. Correlation to clinical and organizational outcomes only.
