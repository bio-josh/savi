THE PROBLEM

Competitive intelligence in biopharma is broken

  • CI teams manually pull data from dozens of disconnected sources
  • Insights arrive weeks or months after competitive events
  • Analysts spend 80% of time on data wrangling, not analysis
  • No single view across Medical Affairs, Commercial, Market Access, Clinical, Regulatory, and Patient Advocacy
OUR SOLUTION

Always-on AI agents that do the work for you

  • Automated ingestion from 15+ public data sources, updated continuously
  • Governed metrics across all six biopharma functions via a semantic layer
  • AI agents that surface insights, not just dashboards that display data
  • Built on a compliance-first architecture — bright-line rules enforced at the data layer

KPIs Across Biopharma Launch Functions

96 KPIs · 6 Functions

One platform that gives Medical, Commercial, and Executive leadership a shared, governed view of the competitive landscape — from L-48 months pre-launch through L+48 post-launch.

🔬

Clinical

12 KPIs · 58% public

Trial portfolio tracking, enrollment velocity, endpoint design analysis, PI overlap detection, and readout monitoring.

Example KPIs: Active trial count by phase, enrollment velocity (pts/site/month), PI publication productivity, efficacy delta vs comparator
🏥

Medical Affairs

30 KPIs · 49% public

Publication velocity, congress share-of-voice, KOL network mapping, MSL deployment tracking, and HEOR contribution scoring.

Example KPIs: Tier-1 journal share of voice, abstract share of voice at ASCO/ESMO, competitive KOL overlap via Sunshine Act, ICER value-based price benchmark
📊

Commercial

14 KPIs · 17% public

Launch readiness signals, prescriber universe sizing, DTC ad detection, revenue vs analyst consensus, and market share proxies.

Example KPIs: Sales force hiring velocity (LinkedIn), brand trademark filing timing, product revenue growth (10-Q), co-pay card launch timing
🏛

Market Access

14 KPIs · 15% public

Formulary coverage tracking, ASP pricing analysis, HTA decision monitoring across ICER/NICE/CADTH/G-BA, and IRA impact modeling.

Example KPIs: Medicare Part D covered-lives %, prior-auth restriction rate, WAC at launch + annual increases, IRA negotiation eligibility clock
📜

Regulatory

15 KPIs · 62% public

FDA approval timelines, expedited designations, AdComm vote tracking, label scope analysis, Orange Book patent monitoring.

Example KPIs: PDUFA date tracking, expedited program designations, approved indication scope vs target, FAERS adverse event signal count
🤝

Patient Advocacy

11 KPIs · 28% public

Advocacy org funding analysis, patient assistance program benchmarking, unmet need scoring from FDA PFDD transcripts.

Example KPIs: Advocacy org scale (IRS 990 revenue), PAP launch timing, co-pay benefit level vs comp set, FDA PFDD meeting mentions
